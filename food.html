FOOD DELIVERY APPLICATION DESIGN



Oct 2022

In October 2022, I designed a user-friendly interface for a food delivery application, showcasing my skills in UI/UX design. I meticulously developed each page using Figma, ensuring a seamless and intuitive user experience. My process included wireframing and prototyping, allowing me to refine the design and create a polished final product. This project highlights my ability to blend creativity with functionality, resulting in a platform that enhances the user's journey from browsing to ordering.
