NETFLIX CLONE



It was a hobby project (may 2023)

I embarked on developing a Netflix clone with a strong emphasis on responsiveness and mobile-friendly design. Leveraging modern frontend development practices with ReactJS, I meticulously crafted a user interface that seamlessly adapts to various screen sizes and devices. The project showcases my commitment to delivering a polished, intuitive experience for users, ensuring that the application is as functional on mobile devices as it is on larger screens. This hands-on experience solidified my expertise in creating responsive web applications that meet the demands of today's diverse digital landscape.
